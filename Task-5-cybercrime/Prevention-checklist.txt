“Stay Safe Online” Checklist:
  Never Enter UPI Pin, to ‘recieve’ money
  Verify VPA Name
  Set Daily Limit
  Avoid “Pay to Work” Scams
  Avoid Logging into confidential accounts, on public wifi
  Don’t Click Shortened Links on Social Media
  2-Factor Authenticate Everything
  If Already Scammed:
    Call the Helpline Number 1930
    Report Online at NCC Reporting Portal: cybercrime.gov.in
    Notify Your Bank
